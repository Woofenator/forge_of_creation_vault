A bridge connecting [[The Borderline]] with the [[Segment of Creation]]. Made of [[Aetherite]] infused rock. Due to the Aetherite infusion, the bridge has magical warding properties, protecting The Forge of Creation from direct threats by either automatically retracting, suddenly opening the floor above a lava pit or creating various other hazards. Seems to have some primitive form of sentience and responds to people being friendly to it, so visitors and locals have created a tradition of greeting the bridge when coming to it and thanking the bridge after crossing.
