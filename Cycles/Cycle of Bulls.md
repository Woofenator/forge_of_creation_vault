Similar to the [[Cycle of the Sea]], this [[Cycle]] is based on seasonal trade. During the Winter months, when the nearby port is inaccessible to ships, goods travel to and from the [[Forge of Creation]] using Bull drawn wagons, thus the name. This trade mostly comes from the Mountains, and consists of Copper, Tin and other metals used for creating tools and contraptions in the city. Those made during this cycle are made of Bronze, and are malleable to various different shapes and tasks. Most of the ones born during this cycle take up fabrication tasks and create most of the trade goods that come out of the City. They also prefer practical applications of various sciences, thus gravitate towards the role of Artificers
