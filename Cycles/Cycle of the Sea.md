Those made in the [[Cycle]] of the Sea are imbued with Silver, leading to a shine to their look. The ones from this cycle are either very diligent and take up high-ranking trade positions, or are lost of their purpose.

The diligent ones take care of their silver look, spending time polishing their frames. A Silver Forged is known through the lands as a diligent and trustworthy merchant, banker, loan shark, book-keeper or any other position associated with the exchange of money and goods

The lost, however, have no interest in their upkeep, thus having their silver frames oxidise, leading to a grey, matte look. They also lack the aptitude for coin, choosing to work instead in the procurement of raw materials in the mines.

Called the Cycle of the Sea due to seasonal trade routes that bring high quality silver to the [[Forge of Creation]], that is then refined further and used for the creation of the Forged
