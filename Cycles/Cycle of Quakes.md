During the [[Cycle]] of Quakes, volcanic activity beneath the [[Forge of Creation]] causes tremors all through out the city. The tremors cause the nearby gold mines to shift and reveal new veins of gold. The first harvest from these new veins is worked into the frame of the Forged created during this cycle. Their frames are not full gold, but exhibit patterns on their otherwise iron bodies. These patterns are not made, but are generated by the [[Aetherite Tools of Creation]]. In public, no-one knows what these patterns represent, or if they even have a rhyme or reason. The golden patterns slightly glow when a Forged with them casts a spell.

Cycle of Quakes forged have a higher aptitude for the arcane and are more in tune with the Weave. This cycle produces by far the most Sourcerors of the population.
