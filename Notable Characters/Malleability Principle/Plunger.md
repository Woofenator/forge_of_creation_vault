One of the oldest Forged in the [[Malleability Principle]], born during the [[Cycle of Bulls]]. Trains recruits in the various underground arts, as well as teaches Deep Speech. At the end of training assigns them their names. Each recruit is given a choice of 3 names that represent their skill set the best, and they choose one.

Also has nicknames for students that are somewhat ironic. 
-  [[Ghost]] `Squeaks` due to him struggling with stealth, particularly in scenarios where the floor could make sounds.
- [[Bug]] is called `Ears` due to not being particularly attentive during spying 101 theory
- [[Shift]] gets the nickname `Stoneface` because they struggled with emotions when impersonating
- [[Radio]] is `Static` - had issues relaying data from one person to the other

He has connections all through the city and his pupils often come to him for help getting contacts for specific jobs, or to procure some supplies on the down low.

Chose the name Plunger as, per his words, "\[He's\] a tool to help shit pass through the system and reach where it's supposed to be"
