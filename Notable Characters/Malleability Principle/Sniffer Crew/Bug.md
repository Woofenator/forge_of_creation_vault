A [[Cycle of the Sea]] forged that skewed the trend and chose to become a spy. Has modified her body to be as compact as possible, thus taking on the look of a Halfling. Is a relatively recently made forged, thus has a lot of emotion and is very animated.

Considers [[Ghost]] as her big brother, as he trained her in stealth while she was struggling with it under the tutelage of [[Plunger]].
