A special faction of the Forged created when nearly 2 dozen were born as Ascendants, something that had not thought possible in the entire time the Forged had been around. The Paragons dominated their piers in skill, technique and mastery, quickly gaining high standing in the Forge and outside of it. While not leaders themselves, they still carry authority, respect and fear. Many have spread out through Nytherra, many gaining favorable positions in the higher echelon's of Sufulear.
